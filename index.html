About This Project

Pakshi Sarvekshan

Abhyuday Samarpan · Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh

Research Question

"Which native bird species of Jabalpur are declining, where are they being sighted, and what environmental factors are responsible?"

This platform builds the local-level data layer that national reports like SoIB 2023 cannot provide. We need to know which mohalla of Jabalpur still has House Sparrows, and whether Bargi Dam's water level is destroying duck nesting sites this season. That's what our citizens can tell us.

Our Approach

01

Clear Research Question

Start with a specific focused question, not vague awareness-raising.

02

Review Existing Research

Every feature informed by SoIB 2023, IUCN data, eBird records, and documented studies.

03

Citizen Data Collection

Every Jabalpuri resident is a potential field researcher.

04

Factor Analysis

We ask not just where is this bird, but what environmental factors are present or absent.

05

Public Data Access

All aggregated findings visible on our dashboard and map.

06

Action

Findings shared with forest authorities and used to advocate for specific interventions.

About Abhyuday Samarpan

Youth-led NGO based in Jabalpur, MP. Our work spans Education, Environment, Animal Welfare, and Swachh Bharat. This platform is part of our environment focus — building evidence-based action.

Data Policy

All sighting data collected voluntarily and stored in a Google Sheet managed by Abhyuday Samarpan. Reporter names and phone numbers never displayed publicly. Aggregated, anonymised data is publicly visible on Dashboard and Map. No tracking. No ads. No third-party data sharing.
